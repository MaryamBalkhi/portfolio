Who am I?

Chasing Many Dreams

Graduate Medical Doctor with over two years of clinical experience, currently working at Milat Hospital in ICU and CCU since August 2025. Previously, I trained at FMIC from January 2023 to February 2024, and in the Emergency Hospital from January 2024 to August 2024, gaining hands-on experience in emergency care, ICU management, and assisting in surgeries, including angiography and stent procedures. I aim to specialize in Internal Medicine, Cardiology, and Emergency Medicine.

In addition to medicine, I am a self-taught cybersecurity enthusiast, enrolled in the EC-Council AI Ethical Hacking course and preparing for international certifications. I also have strong experience in full-stack development, applying my technical skills to real-world projects.

STEM is a major passion of mine. I enjoy building robots, drones, and other space-related machines to deepen my practical knowledge. I continue to self-learn in mechatronics, space engineering, and related fields, aiming to pursue higher studies in the future where I can innovate and contribute to society.

I also aspire to study International Relations with a focus on diplomacy, inspired by Afghanistan’s history and world affairs. I am driven to be a voice for those in need and aim to contribute to leading organizations, merging my medical, STEM, and cybersecurity background to make a meaningful impact and become a model of excellence. I hope to one day pursue opportunities with the EU or NATO.
